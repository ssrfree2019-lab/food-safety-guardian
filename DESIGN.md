# Food Safety Guardian Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Cinematic vertical food-factory drama: deep navy industrial space, stainless steel production geometry, a properly protected Chinese quality professional shown as a grounded working hero, compressed red warning barriers and cold-blue inspection overlays. The emotional arc moves from pressure and uncertainty to decisive professional action and a calm, trustworthy blue-gold resolution.

## Colors

- Deep navy background: `#061525`
- Safety blue: `#28B8FF`
- Cold white: `#F4FAFF`
- Risk red: `#FF3B3B`
- Resolve gold: `#FFC857`

## Typography

- Chinese: Noto Sans SC, 700–900
- Technical labels: Noto Sans SC, 500–700, tabular numerals

## Motion

- Pressure scenes: compressed horizontal movement, micro-shake, hard deceleration.
- Inspection scenes: scanning wipes, measured tracking, controlled reveals.
- Resolution: barriers fragment outward, camera stabilizes, warm light expands.

## What NOT to Do

- No cartoon styling or magical superhero powers.
- No dirty or sensationalized factory imagery.
- No incorrect PPE or exposed hair.
- No dense dashboard UI or tiny unreadable labels.
- No purple neon, rainbow gradients, or generic tech decoration.
